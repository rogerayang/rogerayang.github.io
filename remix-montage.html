Project 1 Remix Montage

This is my photo montage assignment using ten online images and ten of my own images via photography, artwork, and scanned drawings.
The remix montage photo was created using Adobe photoshop and mulitple layers of images overlapping one another to create depth perceptive and a sense of motion and distance.

This photo montage was made on the concept of cultural clash between traditional and modern cultural ideals and styles.
