Narrow Forest Passage

Serpent:",I see the stag has sent you my way, to guide you through this treacherous maze. The pathway is narrow and the woods are tight, for the creatures you shall come across may stay out of sight. For what lives beyond my lair in the night, may take many forms yet has terrifying might. Take the chance if you choose, prepare your last words if you lose. May you hope that you aren't rushing too soon, into a marked grave of certain doom."
