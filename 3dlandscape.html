Project 3 | 3d landscape

An extension of Project 1 the animation project is a combination of photo shop animation based on the images and photo editting used in the photo montage project.
As a result this mp4 video follows the same aesthetic concept as the remix montage focusing on the clash between traditional and modern cultural standards, ideologies and styles.

Initially this video was animated using Adobe photoshop but also used Adobe audition to create and merge the homemade audio recordings used in the video's background.
